Reclaiming Land, Restoring Hope.

Phase 01

Green Soil Remediation

We initiate land recovery with an eco-friendly phytoremediation process. We cultivate native, hyperaccumulator plants like "Hatishur" and "Kalai Grass" directly on the salt-affected soil. These plants act as natural pumps, drawing excess salt from the earth.

After a four-week cycle, the cleansed topsoil is mixed with organic manure and dried green moss, creating a nutrient-rich foundation for crops.

Phase 02

Predictive Spatial Intelligence

To ensure long-term resilience, we integrate cutting-edge technology. Our proprietary spatial intelligence platform uses satellite imagery and data analytics to forecast geographical salt intrusion patterns on a day-by-day timescale.

This predictive capability allows for proactive land management, optimizing remediation efforts and protecting crops before the risk becomes critical. It transforms farming from a reactive to a proactive endeavor.

Phase 03

Sustainable Vertical Farming

On the reclaimed land, we introduce innovative vertical farming systems built from sustainable, locally-sourced bamboo. These structures maximize land efficiency, significantly reduce water consumption, and enable diverse crop cultivation.

This final phase ensures food security and creates resilient agricultural futures, empowering farmers to grow more with less.
